Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly collects only the data you voluntarily provide: expense entries (amount, category, date, description), account credentials for authentication, and basic usage data to improve our service. We do not collect any financial information such as bank account details or credit card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking services you request. This includes storing your expense records, generating spending reports, and providing category-based analysis. We do not use your personal data for advertising or sell your information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure purposes. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing their services to us.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Spendly may use minimal cookies to maintain your login session and improve user experience. We do not use third-party analytics or tracking technologies that profile users.

6. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of any such data, we will delete it promptly.

7. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Spendly after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
